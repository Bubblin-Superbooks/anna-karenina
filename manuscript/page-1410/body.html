She was not the same as she used to be, nor was she as she had been in the carriage; she was quite different.

She was delighted, and so confused at her own delight that there was a moment, the moment when he went up to her sister and glanced again at her, when she, and he, and Dolly, who saw it all, thought she would break down and would begin to cry.
